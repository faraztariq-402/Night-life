Help & Support

Welcome to the help and support page for NiteLyfe! We're here to assist you in making the most of your music experience. Below, you'll find information and resources to help you with any questions or issues you may encounter while using our app.

How Do I Create An Account?

To create an account, follow these steps:

  • Open the app.
  • Tap on "Sign Up" or "Register."
  • Enter your details and follow the on-screen instructions.

How can I change my profile picture?

To change your profile picture:

  • Go to your profile.
  • Tap on your current profile picture.
  • Choose a new picture from your gallery or take a new one.

How can I hire a DJ?

Steps to Hire a DJ:

  • Go to DJ profile.
  • Click "Hire Me."
  • Select an available date and fill out the form.
  • Click "Book Now" to confirm your booking.

Why can't I play some songs?

If you're unable to play certain songs, it could be due to licensing restrictions. Make sure you have a stable internet connection and try again later.
